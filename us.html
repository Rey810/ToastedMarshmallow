What are we?
Imagine not having a “Q” or a “Mr Wonka” you can contact for creative solutions to your problems. We couldn’t. So we’re that.
Meet the team
Leo
Leonida Vitale
Master Crafter
A splendid italian fellow from the East. "How you doing?" Designing and building electronic components is a past time for him. Leo views himself as a 'forever student' who always needs to fulfill his need and ambition to continuously understand new things. Leo has coupled his hobbies and passion with a B.Eng in electrical engineering and has worked in the automation industry to continue to refine his craft. His goal is to help people realise their own ideas or to help others create a solution to their problem by the use of an electronic 'gizmo' or an automated solution.

Tudes

Tudor Wolff
Electronic Wizard
Inspired by the things that cannot be taught, Tudor is our beacon. He inspires us all to find creative solutions to problems, always aiming to impress the people using our products. He loves to hear things like “I can’t believe you thought of that”, “I’m really impressed with how easy this is to use” and “that’s exactly what I had in mind”. As for his own skills, Tudor is an electronic engineer. He is proficient in integrated circuit technology, development and product design. He also supplements the mobile development side of Toasted Marshmallow through his abilities in C# and Swift.

Steve

Steve Purkis
The man on keys
Steve's not been developing all that long. Just over a year now since he started his journey - he has picked up an awful lot in that time though. He's completely aware of how little he knows with regards to developement on the whole - incidentally, it's this notion that pushes him to spending hours a day learning new techniques, reading documentation, doing projects,watching online tutorials and adding tools to his toolbox. He's become very comfortable in his development environment and with his text editor. Steve's most valuable trait as a developer is that he takes absolute pleasure in developing. It enthralls him.
He sincerely believes he can learn anything.
And so he can.
